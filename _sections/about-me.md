Hi!

I am Alok Debnath, an NLP/ML Engineer at Factmata, and an alumnus 
of the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad. 

My work and study primarily
focuses on Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processsing,
though I am now foraying into the allied fields of machine learning and
human computer interaction as well.

My aim as a researcher in this complex field is to find a
computationally viable, mathematically interpretable and linguistically
motivated mechanism of representing, understanding and appreciating
language as a phenomenon. As vague as this sounds, some interesting questions
in this line of thought include: "What is the 'naturalness' of natural language?",
"How can represent extant but underrepresented linguistic phenomena?", and
"What is the most appropriate space whose properties mirror those of language?"
